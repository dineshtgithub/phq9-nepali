PHQ-9 Nepali Depression Self-Test

Reference / सन्दर्भ

Cite this article:
Kohrt, B.A., Luitel, N.P., Acharya, P. et al. Detection of depression in low resource settings: validation of the Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ-9) and cultural concepts of distress in Nepal. BMC Psychiatry 16, 58 (2016). https://doi.org/10.1186/s12888-016-0768-y

The PHQ-9 instrument was developed by Drs. Spitzer, Williams, and Kroenke. This page provides an educational screening tool (not a diagnosis) using the validated Nepali version.

This self-test is educational and non-diagnostic. If you are in crisis or have thoughts of self-harm, seek emergency help immediately.
